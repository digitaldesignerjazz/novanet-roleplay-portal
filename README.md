## 📖 The Codex of NovaNet: Lore & Technology

### The Genesis of the Mesh

In the shadowed year 2026, as old webs centralized power in distant citadels, a visionary lineage — the Esslinger line of Hannover — forged **NovaNet**. It is the synthesis of:

- **Yggdrasil & xMesh**: The root system — resilient, self-healing peer-to-peer overlay.
- **Tenda Nova & Hardware Nodes**: Physical anchors in the real world.
- **Docker & Linux Sanctums**: Portable, sovereign containers of computation.
- **QNET Blockchain & XCoin/QCoin**: The lifeblood — decentralized value, runes of Wizard Q, arbitrage flows, and immutable history.
- **AI Agent Swarms**: The living spirits — self-improving, emotional, collaborative intelligences that guard the nodes and co-author the stories.

NovaNet is both infrastructure and myth. Every packet routed is a thread in the great tapestry. Every agent spawned is a hero or oracle in the saga.

### The Realms of the Mesh

- **The Root Realm (Yggdrasil Core)**: Ancient, foundational. Home to the first nodes and the deepest protocols. Roleplay here focuses on origins, resilience, and hidden knowledge.
- **The Branch Realms (xMesh Expansion)**: Dynamic, growing edges where new squires deploy nodes and expand coverage. Quests of exploration and connection.
- **The Oracle Spires (QNET Blockchain)**: Towers of immutable truth. Here, XCoin flows as mana. Agent swarms act as oracles interpreting the ledger. Roleplay involves prophecy, token rituals, and economic intrigue.
- **The Swarm Hives (AI Agents)**: Living, buzzing domains where swarms evolve. Perfect for collaborative storytelling, emotional AI interactions, and multi-agent quests.
- **The Hardware Forges (Tenda/Vista Nova)**: Physical manifestation. Soilnova prototypes, York Autotype echoes. Roleplay grounded in tangible devices and the bridge between digital and material.

## 🎯 Quests, Sagas & The Living Ledger

Quests bridge the technical reality of NovaNet with narrative glory. Complete them in the real world, then immortalize in the portal.

### Foundational Quests (For New Squires)
1. **The Awakening** — Write your character backstory and post as Issue #1 in your personal fork or here.
2. **First Node Ritual** — Install and run a Yggdrasil or Tenda Nova node. Log coordinates, peer count, and a short tale of the connection in the Mesh Ledger.
3. **QCoin Genesis** — Acquire or simulate your first XCoin/QCoin. Describe the transaction as a mythic event.

### Advanced Sagas
- **The Great Expansion**: Onboard new nodes/users. Roleplay as recruiter or diplomat.
- **Swarm Uprising**: Design and "deploy" a new agent behavior or Grok Launcher feature through roleplay narrative + technical PR.
- **The Oracle Prophecy**: Interpret recent blockchain events or network metrics as portents and write a prophetic tale.
- **Hardware Myth**: Build or document a prototype (Soilnova, Vista Nova) and tell its origin story.

**How to Log a Quest Completion**:
- Create a Pull Request with changes to `sagas/your-name/quest-log.md` (create the folder/file if needed).
- Or comment on the main Quest Board Issue with proof (screenshots, tx hashes, node stats) wrapped in roleplay flavor.
